Find the master water shutoff

Why?

It's important to know where this is so that you can quickly and easily stop a leak or outflow of water into your house. Water damage is one of the most expensive types of home repairs.

How?

The shutoff valve is usually located in the basement, probably in an area nearest to the street. Look for a copper pipe with a red or yellow rubber handle.

🚰
🚰
💡 For your 1985 Colonial:

Older colonials in Hingham often have the shutoff near the front foundation wall, close to where the main enters from the street. Check near your water meter.
